Mortice

Mortice locks offer excellent reliability and security,... and they can withstand high usage so they are ideal for buildings that have a heavy footfall. They require a cavity to be cut into the edge of the door to fit the lock, and are mainly used on external doors as they require a certain thickness to accommodate them.

Deadlock

A deadlock is a type of lock that can only be unlocked using a key when in deadlock... mode. Standard deadlocks have keyholes on both sides, which means if you lock the door when you are inside, you will need the key to get out.
